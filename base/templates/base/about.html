About Company


We offer long and short-term car rental deals for private customers, with or without drivers with unlimited mileage all over the Mindanao area. We also provide car delivery and car pick up all the way to the customer's house.


Why Choose Us?

  • If you want to book directly through a supplier, and not through a broker - choose McQueens Car Rental
  • This will give you better flexibility in terms of vehicle choices
  • Vehicle make and model will be confirmed, and not “similar” to those you selected
  • You can directly negotiate some of the terms and conditions, payment options, especially if you require unique or long term rental service

    • We offer a varied fleet of cars, ranging car brands from Toyota, Nissan, Suzuki, Kia, Hyunda, and Honda. All our vehicles have air conditioning power steering, electric windows. All our vehicles are bought and maintained at official dealerships only. Automatic transmission cars are available in every booking class. We pride ourselves on personalized service, great cars and excellent rates.
